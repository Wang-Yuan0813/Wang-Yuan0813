<h2>Before Dawn 1  
<span>

</span>
  🔗<a href="https://github.com/Wang-Yuan0813/Netease-GameProject-1">Repository</a> </h2>
<p>
  
该项目是24年参加网易Y3游戏制作大赛的冠军作品，后续添加商业化道具并上架游戏平台，截止目前已盈利超过5000￥。该项目采用Y3编辑器（Lua）进行开发，融入了技能树、地图探索、俯视角射击、即时策略等元素，并加入《Astroneer》中的氧气桩链接机制，玩家需要扮演一位普通的小兵，在虫族一波一波的进攻中保护基地并升级技能树与兵种，最终一举推翻虫族老巢。
  
This project was the championship-winning entry in the 2024 NetEase Y3 Game Development Competition. It was later enhanced with monetization features and published on a gaming platform, generating over ¥5,000 in revenue to date. The game was developed using the Y3 Editor (Lua) and incorporates elements such as a skill tree, map exploration, top-down shooting, and real-time strategy. It also integrates the oxygen tether connection mechanic inspired by Astroneer. Players take on the role of an ordinary soldier, defending a base against waves of insectoid enemies, upgrading skills and units along the skill tree, and ultimately launching a decisive assault to overthrow the enemy hive.

<h2>Before Dawn 2  
<span>
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/-Lua-2C2D72?style=flat-square&logo=lua&logoColor=white"/>
</span>
  🔗<a href="https://github.com/Wang-Yuan0813/Netease-GameProject-2">Repository</a> </h2>
<p>
  
在一代的基础上，本作加入了局外仓库、肉鸽抽卡、多人联机等系统，最多支持四名玩家同时游玩，采用帧同步作为网络同步方式。玩家可以通过在局内击败敌人获取局外材料，并在局外进行升级和新角色的解锁。玩家扮演镇压虫族的指挥官，带领小兵，发挥特殊技能，击败不断来袭的虫族大军。
  
Building on the first installment, this sequel introduces systems such as an out-of-run inventory, roguelike-style gacha mechanics, and multiplayer support for up to four players, using frame synchronization for networking. Players can defeat enemies during runs to obtain materials, which can then be used outside of runs to upgrade abilities and unlock new characters. Taking on the role of a commander tasked with suppressing the insectoid forces, players lead squads of soldiers, utilize unique skills, and fend off relentless waves of enemies before ultimately launching a final assault against the enemy hive.
  
</p>
